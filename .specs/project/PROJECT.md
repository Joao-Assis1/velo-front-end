# Velo Frontend - Driving School Management System

## Vision
To create an intuitive, responsive frontend for a driving school management system that streamlines operations for both students and instructors, providing seamless scheduling, progress tracking, and administrative capabilities.

## Goals
1. **User Experience**: Provide a clean, modern interface that's easy to navigate for both students and instructors
2. **Functionality**: Enable complete lifecycle management from registration to course completion
3. **Performance**: Deliver fast load times and smooth interactions
4. **Scalability**: Build with maintainable architecture that can grow with feature additions
5. **Accessibility**: Ensure the application is usable by people with diverse abilities

## Success Criteria
- Students can easily schedule classes, track progress, and manage their profile
- Instructors can manage their availability, view student lists, and track earnings
- Administrative functions (though not yet implemented in frontend) are designed for easy integration
- Application is responsive across device sizes
- Code follows established conventions and is well-tested

## Target Users
- **Students**: Individuals learning to drive who need to schedule lessons, track progress, and manage their journey
- **Instructors**: Certified driving instructors who manage their schedule, view assigned students, and track their teaching activities

## Stakeholders
- Driving school administrators (backend focus)
- Frontend development team
- UI/UX designers
- Quality assurance team

## Timeline
- Phase 1: Core authentication and role-based navigation (Weeks 1-2)
- Phase 2: Student dashboard and scheduling (Weeks 3-4)
- Phase 3: Instructor dashboard and management (Weeks 5-6)
- Phase 4: Progress tracking and reporting (Weeks 7-8)
- Phase 5: Payment processing and advanced features (Weeks 9-10)

## Non-Goals
- Backend development (assumed to be handled separately)
- Mobile native applications (web-responsive approach)
- Advanced analytics dashboard (future enhancement)
